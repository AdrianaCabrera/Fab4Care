Final Project

For this Thursday please bring the following points:

  1. Think of a title for your project.
  2. This project is an assistive device, so it needs to be a system composed of sensing and actuation. (we will work on it in the next days)
  3. This device is intended to be wearable or to fit the person ( personalized ).
  4. DIY, The prototype of this project must be initialised in the FabLab.
  5. This device needs to be aesthetically pleasing.
  6. For your final projects, please bring sketches of your ideas, scenarios where some of the patients may be involved, look for relative works all existing projects in the scientific and commercial field.
  7. Don’t forget to document it in your website ❤ ☺!
